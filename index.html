📖 About Helping To Rise

Helping To Rise is a pure community-driven giving platform — not an investment scheme or "get rich quick" project.

It was created so that successful people (creators, entrepreneurs, influencers) can easily give back to their followers and supporters who helped them rise.

Because borders and currencies divide us, there was no simple global way for people to easily and directly help each other — so this community was born.

Core Rules:

1. It is 100% about helping each other. { Volunteer Service }<
2. To receive help, your external wallet only needs a one-time minimum of $1 worth of RISE (this is not taken from you — it is just to stop bots, phone farms, and scammers).<
3. To give help, you simply buy TON or RISE token and donate it through the App. The system generates a code to give out.
4. Everything is built on 100% Integrity, Transparency, and Real People.

Scam Warning:
This is NOT an Investment App. We DO NOT encourage buying bulk RISE tokens to "keep and become rich."<
Beware of anyone using the Helping To Rise name for fraud and scams.
NO AI or animated content appears in our official social media.
